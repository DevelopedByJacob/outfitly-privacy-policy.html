Information We Collect Outfitly is designed to help you organize outfits, clothing items and personal profiles on your device. You may add photos by taking a picture with your device camera or by selecting an existing image from your device gallery. These photos are used only to provide the app’s core functionality, such as displaying and organizing your wardrobe and outfits. Unless explicitly stated otherwise inside the app, photos added to Outfitly are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to servers controlled by developedbyjacob. ### App profiles and outfit data Outfitly may allow you to create multiple local profiles, especially for Premium users.
